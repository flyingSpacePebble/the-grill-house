ABOUT

When we opened The Grill House in May 2015, our vision was to create an unassuming watering hole for the locals. In a town with endless options for bars and lounges, we found it challenging to find a friendly bar with a solid craft beer list, good food, and non-gaming/non-smoking. We wanted to provide friends and neighbors alike with the original form of social networking; a fun easy going bar, and a cold pint. Our staunch belief in providing exceptional quality products with friendly service in a clean and welcoming setting has helped us earn the reputation among the locals as one of the best hidden gems in Los Angeles.

The Grill House is the culmination of giving our best to the community by providing great food, service, and atmosphere. It allows us an opportunity to create a memorable setting for you to catch up with friends and make new ones over tasty bites and a refreshing drink.
